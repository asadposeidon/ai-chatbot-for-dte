About the AI Chatbot

The AI Chatbot for the Department of Technical Education is built to assist students and staff with quick, accurate information about academic processes and services. Instead of searching through documents or waiting in queues, users can simply ask a question and get an instant response.

The system helps students access details related to admissions, course structures, examination schedules, scholarship programs, and key department policies—reducing manual workload for staff and improving accessibility to information across the department.

2

3
